New Haven, CT 06510

I am a data scientist and software engineer in the Department of Biomedical Informatics & Data Science at Yale University, where I have the great privilege of working with Qingyu Chen and Hua Xu. I hold a Master’s degree in Physics and a Master’s degree in Data Science from the University of Kentucky, where I was fortunate to be advised by Ramakanth Kavuluru during my second Master’s.

My research focuses on advancing AI in healthcare, particularly in:

  • End-to-end information extraction
  • Ophthalmology-specific (multimodal) LLMs
  • RAG and agent systems in healthcare
  • AI development leveraging real-world data (RWD) and real-world evidence (RWE), such as the OMOP Common Data Model
